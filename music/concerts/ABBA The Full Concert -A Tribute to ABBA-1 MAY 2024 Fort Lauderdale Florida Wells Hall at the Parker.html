Concert Details

Artist: Tribute to ABBA Ensemble

Concert Name: ABBA The Full Concert - A Tribute to ABBA

Year: 2024

Venue: Lillian S. Wells Hall at The Parker (Fort Lauderdale, Florida)

Date: May 1, 2024

Background & Production Notes

This performance captures a beautifully staged, high-energy live celebration dedicated entirely to the timeless catalogue of Swedish pop royalty, ABBA. Recorded on May 1, 2024, at the historic Lillian S. Wells Hall inside The Parker in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, the production treats fans to an authentic, theater-grade concert experience filled with nostalgic choreography, vibrant costume changes, and stellar musicianship.

The venue's warm acoustics provide the perfect backdrop for replicating ABBA’s famous multi-layered vocal structures and intricate studio wall-of-sound production. The concert spans the group's legendary golden era, delivering highly accurate live renditions of massive worldwide hits using period-accurate instrumentation and dynamic staging.

Performance Highlights

The ensemble delivers a brilliant chronological journey through pop history, beautifully carrying the audience from early Eurovision triumphs up to the peak disco explosion of the late 1970s. The crowd dynamic inside The Parker reflects the universal appeal of the music, featuring massive auditorium sing-alongs during definitive showstoppers like "Dancing Queen," "Mamma Mia," and "Waterloo."

Special attention is paid to the distinct stage personas and iconic vocal arrangements originally established by Agnetha, Björn, Benny, and Anni-Frid, giving attendees a true-to-form retro experience.

Fun Facts & Trivia

  • The concert took place at The Parker, a fully reimagined cultural landmark theater in Fort Lauderdale celebrated for its intimate setting and pristine sound engineering.
  • The performance features flawless live execution of ABBA's signature complex dual-female lead vocal harmonies.
  • True to style, the stage costumes worn during the show are direct replicas of the flashing satin, glitter, and platform boots worn by the original lineup during their iconic 1977 and 1979 world tours.
  • The set configuration blends high-tempo dance block anthems perfectly alongside moving, stripped-down piano ballads.

Spotlight

The Ensemble — Featuring precise dual keyboards, driving guitars, and striking live vocals meticulously configured to capture the distinct pop-perfection essence of original ABBA classics.
