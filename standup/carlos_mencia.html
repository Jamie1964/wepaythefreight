Biography

Carlos Mencia is a Honduran-American stand‑up comedian known for his bold, high‑energy comedy style and his hit Comedy Central series Mind of Mencia. His material often focuses on culture, race, society, and everyday absurdities. Mencia has toured globally, released multiple specials, and remains a recognizable figure in the comedy world.
