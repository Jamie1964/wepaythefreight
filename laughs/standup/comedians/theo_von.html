Biography

Theo Von is an American stand‑up comedian, podcaster, and storyteller known for his unique Southern charm, surreal humor, and one‑of‑a‑kind life experiences.

Born and raised in Louisiana, Theo’s comedy blends wild childhood stories, philosophical rambling, and heartfelt honesty. His Netflix special No Offense and his hit podcast This Past Weekend have made him one of the most recognizable voices in modern comedy.

With a massive online following and a touring schedule that sells out nationwide, Theo Von continues to rise as one of the most original comedic voices of his generation.
