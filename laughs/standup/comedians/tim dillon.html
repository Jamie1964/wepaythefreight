Biography

Tim Dillon is an American stand‑up comedian, podcaster, and cultural commentator known for his explosive rants, sharp satire, and fearless takes on politics, media, and modern society.

His Netflix special A Real Hero and his hit podcast The Tim Dillon Show have earned him a massive global following. Dillon’s comedy blends conspiracy‑theory absurdity, social commentary, and high‑energy storytelling.

With a touring schedule that sells out theaters nationwide, Tim Dillon has become one of the most influential and unpredictable voices in contemporary comedy.
