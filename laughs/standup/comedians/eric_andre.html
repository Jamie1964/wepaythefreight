Biography

Eric Andre is an American comedian, actor, and creator best known for his surreal, chaotic, and boundary‑breaking comedy. He rose to fame as the host of The Eric Andre Show, a wildly unpredictable anti‑talk‑show blending pranks, absurdity, and shock humor.

Andre’s stand‑up is just as unhinged — a mix of physical comedy, bizarre storytelling, and fearless experimentation. He has appeared in films, TV shows, and voice roles, including The Lion King (2019), Bad Trip, and Man Seeking Woman.

With a cult following and a reputation for pushing comedy to its limits, Eric Andre remains one of the most original and unpredictable performers working today.
