Biography

Adam Sandler is an American comedian, actor, writer, and musician known for his energetic stand‑up, unforgettable characters, and a long career of hit comedy films. From his early days on Saturday Night Live to blockbuster movies and Netflix specials, Sandler remains one of the most recognizable and influential comedic entertainers of his generation.
