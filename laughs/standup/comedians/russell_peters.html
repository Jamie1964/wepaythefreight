Biography

Russell Peters is a Canadian stand‑up comedian and global superstar known for his lightning‑fast crowd work, cultural humor, and pitch‑perfect accents. His breakout special Comedy Now! became one of the first stand‑up sets to go viral online, launching him into international fame.

Peters has sold out arenas worldwide — including Madison Square Garden and London’s O2 Arena — making him one of the highest‑grossing touring comedians of all time.

His comedy blends cultural observations, family stories, and rapid‑fire improvisation, earning him fans across every continent.
