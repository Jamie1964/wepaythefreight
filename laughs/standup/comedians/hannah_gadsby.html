Biography

Hannah Gadsby is an award‑winning Australian comedian, writer, and performer known for their groundbreaking blend of stand‑up, storytelling, and social commentary. Gadsby rose to global fame with the Netflix special Nanette, which redefined modern comedy and earned widespread critical acclaim.

Their follow‑up specials, including Douglas and Something Special, continued to showcase Gadsby’s sharp wit, vulnerability, and unique comedic voice. Their work often explores identity, trauma, art history, and LGBTQ+ experiences.

With a powerful stage presence and a reputation for pushing the boundaries of stand‑up, Gadsby remains one of the most influential comedic storytellers of this generation.
