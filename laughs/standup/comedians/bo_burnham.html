Biography

Bo Burnham is an American comedian, writer, director, musician, and filmmaker known for his innovative, genre‑bending approach to comedy. He began his career on YouTube as a teenager, quickly gaining millions of views for his musical comedy and sharp, introspective humor.

Burnham’s specials — including what., Make Happy, and the groundbreaking Inside — blend music, performance art, and emotional depth, earning him widespread critical acclaim. Inside became a cultural phenomenon, praised for its creativity, honesty, and cinematic style.

Beyond stand‑up, Burnham has written and directed films such as Eighth Grade and contributed to numerous creative projects across comedy, music, and film.
