Biography

Josh Blue is an American stand‑up comedian known for his sharp wit, fearless honesty, and brilliantly self‑aware humor. He rose to national fame after winning Last Comic Standing, where his unique comedic voice and physical storytelling captivated audiences.

Blue often incorporates his experience with cerebral palsy into his act, using humor to challenge stereotypes, break barriers, and connect with audiences on a deeply human level. His comedy blends personal stories, physical comedy, and quick improvisation.

With a massive following and a reputation as one of the most original voices in stand‑up, Josh Blue continues to tour internationally and release new specials.
