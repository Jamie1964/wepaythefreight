Biography

Mark Normand is an American stand‑up comedian known for his rapid‑fire delivery, razor‑sharp writing, and classic New York comedy style. His jokes are tight, punchy, and packed with misdirection, making him one of the most respected joke‑writers working today.

Normand has released multiple acclaimed specials, including Out to Lunch and Soup to Nuts, both praised for their nonstop laughs and airtight structure. He has appeared on major late‑night shows, podcasts, and comedy festivals worldwide.

With a massive online following and a reputation as one of the most prolific joke machines in modern stand‑up, Mark Normand continues to tour internationally and release new material.
