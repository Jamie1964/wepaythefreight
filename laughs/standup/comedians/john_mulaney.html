Biography

John Mulaney is an American stand‑up comedian, writer, and actor known for his sharp wit, polished delivery, and masterful storytelling. He first gained widespread recognition as a writer on Saturday Night Live, where he co‑created the beloved character Stefon with Bill Hader.

Mulaney’s stand‑up specials — including New in Town, The Comeback Kid, and Kid Gorgeous — have earned critical acclaim for their clever writing, memorable bits, and unique comedic rhythm.

With a massive fanbase and a reputation as one of the most influential comedians of his generation, John Mulaney continues to tour internationally and release new specials.
