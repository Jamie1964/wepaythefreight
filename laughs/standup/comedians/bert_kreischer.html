Biography

Bert Kreischer is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, and podcast host known for his wild storytelling, high‑energy stage presence, and signature shirtless performances. His legendary story “The Machine” became one of the most viral comedy bits of all time and later inspired a feature film in which he starred.

Kreischer has released multiple stand‑up specials, including Razzle Dazzle, Hey Big Boy, and Secret Time, each showcasing his ability to turn chaotic life experiences into unforgettable comedy. He also co‑hosts two major podcasts: 2 Bears, 1 Cave with Tom Segura and Bertcast.

With a massive fanbase and a reputation for outrageous humor, Kreischer continues to be one of the most recognizable and beloved comedians in the world.
