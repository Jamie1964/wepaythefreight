Biography

Jim Gaffigan is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, and bestselling author known for his clean humor, food‑obsessed jokes, and iconic “inner voice” delivery. His comedy often centers on parenting, laziness, food, and the absurdities of everyday life.

Gaffigan has released numerous successful stand‑up specials, including Beyond the Pale, Mr. Universe, Cinco, and Quality Time. He has also appeared in films, television, and his own semi‑autobiographical sitcom The Jim Gaffigan Show.

With a massive global fanbase and a reputation as one of the most successful clean comedians in history, Jim Gaffigan continues to tour internationally and release new specials.
