Biography

Carrot Top (Scott Thompson) is an American stand‑up comedian known for his high‑energy performances, prop‑based comedy, and unmistakable bright red hair. Rising to fame in the 1990s, he quickly became one of the most recognizable comedians in the world thanks to his inventive visual gags and explosive stage presence.

He has maintained one of the longest‑running comedy residencies in Las Vegas, performing at the Luxor Hotel for over a decade. His act blends physical comedy, rapid‑fire jokes, and a massive rotating collection of props that he designs himself.

With a career spanning more than 30 years, Carrot Top remains a unique and iconic figure in modern comedy.
