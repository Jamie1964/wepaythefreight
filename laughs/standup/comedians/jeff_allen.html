Biography

Jeff Allen is an American stand‑up comedian known for his clean, family‑friendly humor and deeply relatable storytelling. With a career spanning over four decades, Allen has become one of the most respected voices in comedy, performing on major stages, television specials, and national tours.

His comedy often focuses on marriage, parenting, everyday frustrations, and the absurdities of modern life. Allen’s signature bit, “Happy Wife, Happy Life,” became a cultural catchphrase and helped cement his place in comedy history.

With a loyal fanbase and a reputation for heartfelt, uplifting humor, Jeff Allen continues to inspire and entertain audiences across the country.
