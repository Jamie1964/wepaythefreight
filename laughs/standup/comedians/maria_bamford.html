Biography

Maria Bamford is an American stand‑up comedian, actress, and voice performer known for her surreal humor, emotional honesty, and unmatched ability to shift between characters and voices. Her comedy often explores mental health, family, anxiety, and the absurdity of everyday life.

Bamford has released multiple acclaimed specials, including The Special Special Special! and Old Baby, both celebrated for their originality and vulnerability. She also starred in the Netflix series Lady Dynamite, a semi‑autobiographical comedy about her life and recovery.

With a devoted fanbase and a reputation as one of the most innovative comedic voices of her generation, Maria Bamford continues to tour, act, and create groundbreaking work.
