Biography

Nate Bargatze is an American stand‑up comedian known for his clean humor, slow‑burn delivery, and brilliantly understated storytelling. His comedy often centers on everyday misunderstandings, family life, and the absurdity of being a regular guy in a complicated world.

Bargatze has released multiple acclaimed specials, including The Tennessee Kid, Greatest Average American, and Hello World. His laid‑back style and universal relatability have earned him a massive fanbase and widespread critical praise.

With a reputation as one of the most consistent joke writers in modern stand‑up, Nate Bargatze continues to tour internationally and host his popular podcast, NateLand.
