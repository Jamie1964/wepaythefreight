Biography

Andrew Santino is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, and podcaster known for his sharp wit, quick improvisation, and fiery comedic style. His stand‑up blends personal stories, observational humor, and a signature sarcastic edge that has made him a favorite in clubs and theaters across the country.

Santino has appeared in hit shows like Dave, Mixology, and I'm Dying Up Here, showcasing his range as both a comedian and actor. He also co‑hosts the massively popular podcast Bad Friends with Bobby Lee, along with his solo podcast Whiskey Ginger.

With a growing fanbase and a reputation for high‑energy performances, Santino continues to rise as one of the most dynamic comedic voices working today.
