Biography

Demetri Martin is an American stand‑up comedian, writer, actor, and artist known for his clever one‑liners, visual humor, and unique use of drawings and music in his performances. His cerebral, minimalist style sets him apart, blending wordplay, diagrams, and deadpan delivery into a signature comedic voice.

Martin gained national attention through his work on The Daily Show and later with his own series, Important Things with Demetri Martin. He has released multiple specials, written books, and even directed films, showcasing his wide creative range.

With a loyal fanbase and a reputation for smart, inventive comedy, Martin continues to be one of the most distinctive performers in modern stand‑up.
