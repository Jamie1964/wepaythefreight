Biography

Mike Epps is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, and producer known for his charismatic stage presence, sharp observational humor, and unforgettable roles in film and television. He gained widespread recognition for his role as Day‑Day in the Friday film series, becoming a fan favorite for his comedic timing and expressive delivery.

Epps has released multiple stand‑up specials, starred in major films, and continues to be a powerhouse in both comedy and acting. His style blends storytelling, physical comedy, and cultural commentary with a smooth, confident swagger.

With a massive fanbase and decades of experience, Mike Epps remains one of the most influential and recognizable comedians working today.
