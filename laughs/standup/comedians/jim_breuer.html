Biography

Jim Breuer is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, and radio personality known for his high‑energy delivery, expressive physical comedy, and unforgettable characters. He rose to fame as a cast member on Saturday Night Live, where he became iconic for his “Goat Boy” character and spot‑on impressions.

Breuer has starred in films such as Half Baked, released multiple stand‑up specials, and built a massive following through his storytelling‑driven comedy. His material often covers family life, aging, rock music, and the absurdities of everyday experiences.

With decades of touring and a reputation as one of the most animated performers in stand‑up, Jim Breuer continues to entertain audiences worldwide.
