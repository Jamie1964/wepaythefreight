Biography

Gilbert Gottfried was an American stand‑up comedian, actor, and voice performer known for his unmistakable voice, fearless delivery, and boundary‑pushing humor. His career spanned over 40 years, making him one of the most recognizable and influential comedic voices of all time.

He appeared in films, television, and countless roasts, and was beloved for voicing Iago the parrot in Disney’s Aladdin. His stand‑up was bold, unapologetic, and often shocking — exactly what his fans adored.

Gottfried also hosted the popular Gilbert Gottfried’s Amazing Colossal Podcast, where he interviewed legends of film and comedy. His legacy continues to inspire comedians around the world.
