Biography

Drew Lynch is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, and content creator who rose to national fame after his unforgettable run on America’s Got Talent, where he earned the Golden Buzzer and became a fan favorite. Known for his sharp wit, self‑aware humor, and heartfelt storytelling, Lynch blends vulnerability with punchlines in a way that resonates deeply with audiences.

After developing a stutter from a sports injury, Lynch transformed his life experience into a powerful comedic voice. His YouTube channel, podcast, and live shows have built him a massive global following.

With millions of fans and a unique comedic perspective, Drew Lynch continues to be one of the most inspiring and entertaining performers in modern stand‑up.
