Biography

Trevor Noah is a South African comedian, author, and former host of The Daily Show, known for his sharp global perspective, storytelling brilliance, and ability to blend humor with cultural insight.

His stand‑up specials — including Son of Patricia, Afraid of the Dark, and I Wish You Would — showcase his unique ability to turn personal experiences into universal comedy.

With a massive international following and a touring schedule that spans continents, Trevor Noah remains one of the most influential comedic voices in the world.
