Biography

George Lopez is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, and television host known for his sharp observational humor, cultural commentary, and groundbreaking sitcom George Lopez. His comedy often explores family, identity, and the Latino experience, making him one of the most influential Latino comedians of all time.
