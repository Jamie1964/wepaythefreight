Biography

Steve Harvey is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, author, and television host whose career spans over three decades. Rising to fame through the legendary Original Kings of Comedy tour, Harvey became known for his sharp observational humor, charismatic stage presence, and storytelling rooted in family, relationships, and everyday life.

Beyond stand‑up, Harvey is the host of Family Feud, Judge Steve Harvey, and The Steve Harvey Morning Show, making him one of the most recognizable entertainers in the world.

His influence extends into books, motivational speaking, and philanthropy, solidifying him as a multi‑platform icon.
