Biography

Tony Hinchcliffe is an American stand‑up comedian, writer, and host known for his razor‑sharp roasting style and fearless comedic delivery.

He gained national attention as a writer for the Comedy Central Roast series and later became the creator and host of the wildly popular live show and podcast Kill Tony, where comedians perform one‑minute sets followed by rapid‑fire critiques.

With a massive fanbase and a touring schedule that sells out worldwide, Tony Hinchcliffe has become one of the most influential voices in modern stand‑up and live comedy formats.
