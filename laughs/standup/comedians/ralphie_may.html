Biography

Ralphie May was an American stand‑up comedian known for his fearless honesty, massive stage presence, and ability to tackle taboo topics with warmth, charm, and razor‑sharp timing. His breakout came on Last Comic Standing, launching a career filled with specials, tours, and a devoted fanbase.

May’s comedy blended Southern storytelling, cultural commentary, and personal vulnerability, making him one of the most beloved and influential comedians of his generation.

His legacy continues through his specials, recordings, and the millions of fans who still revisit his work today.
