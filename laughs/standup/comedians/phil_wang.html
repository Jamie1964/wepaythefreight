Biography

Phil Wang is a British-Malaysian stand‑up comedian known for his sharp writing, intellectual humor, and uniquely charming blend of cultural commentary and absurdity. His comedy often explores identity, heritage, relationships, and the quirks of modern life.

Wang has released acclaimed specials, including Philly Philly Wang Wang on Netflix, and is a regular on major UK panel shows such as Taskmaster, Live at the Apollo, and Would I Lie to You?

With a growing international fanbase and a reputation as one of the smartest voices in modern stand‑up, Phil Wang continues to tour globally and release new work across platforms.
