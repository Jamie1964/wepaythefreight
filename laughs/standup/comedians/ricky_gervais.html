Biography

Ricky Gervais is an English stand‑up comedian, actor, writer, and creator known for his fearless humor, sharp social commentary, and groundbreaking work in television. He co‑created and starred in The Office, one of the most influential comedies of all time, and later created Extras, Derek, and After Life.

As a stand‑up, Gervais is known for his unapologetic, provocative style, blending dark humor, philosophy, and cultural critique. His specials — including Humanity, SuperNature, and Armageddon — have earned global acclaim.

With a massive international following and a reputation as one of the most influential comedic voices of the 21st century, Ricky Gervais continues to tour, write, and create new work.
