Biography

Ali Wong is an American stand‑up comedian, writer, and actress known for her fearless honesty, sharp wit, and groundbreaking Netflix specials. Her breakout special Baby Cobra — filmed while seven months pregnant — became a cultural phenomenon and introduced audiences to her bold, unfiltered comedic voice.

Wong followed with Hard Knock Wife and Don Wong, each expanding her reputation as one of the most original and influential comedians of her generation. Beyond stand‑up, she co‑created and starred in the hit series Beef, appeared in films, and authored the bestselling book Dear Girls.

Her comedy blends personal stories, cultural commentary, and unapologetic humor, making her one of the most recognizable comedic voices working today.
