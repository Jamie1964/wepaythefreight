Biography

Craig Robinson is an American comedian, actor, and musician best known for his unforgettable role as Darryl Philbin on The Office. His comedy blends soulful music, improvisation, and playful crowd interaction, creating a unique live experience that mixes stand‑up with piano‑driven musical comedy.

Robinson has appeared in hit films such as Hot Tub Time Machine, This Is the End, Pineapple Express, and Sausage Party. His smooth delivery, musical talent, and charismatic stage presence have made him a favorite in clubs, theaters, and festivals worldwide.

Whether performing solo or with his band “The Nasty Delicious,” Craig brings a warm, joyful, and high‑energy comedic style that audiences love.
