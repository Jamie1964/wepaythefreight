Biography

Eddie Griffin is an American comedian, actor, and writer known for his fearless delivery, sharp social commentary, and explosive stage presence. Rising to fame in the 1990s, Griffin became a standout voice in comedy with his bold takes on culture, politics, and everyday life.

He starred in the hit sitcom Malcolm & Eddie, delivered iconic performances in films like Undercover Brother and Deuce Bigalow, and released multiple acclaimed stand‑up specials. His raw, unapologetic style has earned him a loyal global fanbase.

With decades of experience and a reputation as one of the most dynamic performers in comedy, Griffin continues to tour and create new material that pushes boundaries and keeps audiences laughing.
