Biography

Jerry Seinfeld is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, writer, and producer best known for co‑creating and starring in the legendary sitcom Seinfeld, often regarded as one of the greatest television shows of all time. His comedy is rooted in observational humor, focusing on the absurdities of everyday life.

Seinfeld began his stand‑up career in the late 1970s and quickly became a staple of the comedy circuit. His clean, precise delivery and sharp insights earned him a massive following. Beyond stand‑up, he created and hosted the hit series Comedians in Cars Getting Coffee.

With decades of influence and a reputation as one of the most iconic comedians in history, Jerry Seinfeld continues to perform, write, and shape the world of comedy.
