Biography

Jim Jefferies is an Australian stand‑up comedian, writer, and actor known for his bold, unfiltered comedy and sharp social commentary. His storytelling style, combined with his willingness to tackle controversial topics, has made him one of the most influential comedians of the modern era.

Jefferies gained international fame with his specials Bare, Freedumb, and This Is Me Now, and later hosted the Comedy Central series The Jim Jefferies Show, where he blended humor with political and cultural analysis.

With a massive global fanbase and a reputation for fearless honesty, Jim Jefferies continues to tour internationally and release new specials.
