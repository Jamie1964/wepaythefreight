Biography

Dane Cook is an American stand‑up comedian and actor known for his high‑energy storytelling, animated delivery, and massive influence during the 2000s comedy boom. His albums Harmful If Swallowed and Retaliation became some of the best‑selling comedy records of all time, helping him break into mainstream stardom.

Cook was one of the first comedians to leverage social media to build a huge fanbase, selling out arenas including Madison Square Garden. He has appeared in films such as Employee of the Month, Good Luck Chuck, and My Best Friend’s Girl.

With decades of touring, multiple specials, and a dedicated global audience, Dane remains a major figure in modern stand‑up comedy.
