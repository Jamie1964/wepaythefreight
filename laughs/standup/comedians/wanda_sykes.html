Biography

Wanda Sykes is an American stand‑up comedian, actress, and writer known for her sharp wit, fearless social commentary, and unmistakable comedic voice.

She first gained national attention writing for The Chris Rock Show, earning an Emmy Award. Her stand‑up specials — including Not Normal and I’ma Be Me — showcase her bold, insightful, and hilarious takes on politics, culture, and everyday life.

With decades of experience across TV, film, and stand‑up, Wanda Sykes remains one of the most respected and influential comedians of her generation.
